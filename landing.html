About Us
Viveka Bharathi Society's

Vivekananda Vidya Kendra(VVK) is a tuition center with ALL-ROUND EDUCATION

Popularly known as VVK Warangal

LOCATION: Kashibugga, Warangal, Telangana

Our daily activities are as follows

 Morning (6AM - 8AM)

Yoga/Sports/Martial Arts/Chess, Carrom/Drawing/Handwriting/Library book reading/News.....

Evening (6PM - 9PM)

Prayer - Slokha Chanting, Story Telling

Studies - Tuition class, study hours, homework, literacy improvement....

Sunday special (5PM - 8PM)

Movie show - movies based on real-life stories, Bhajans

Success Stories

Success Stories of 10th class students in 2024

30 students of 10th class, from two government schools joined in our VVK

2 school toppers from 2 government schools in this year SSC results

10 out of 30 got 9 and above GPA (100% pass)

Yoga practice, dance performances, sports, martial arts, Slokha chanting, bhajans, movie shows... were part of their year-long curriculum.

Vivekananda Vidya Kendra (VVK) is a center for local govt. school students who couldn't afford to learn Sports, Dance, Yoga along with character building education including academic tuition classes. It runs on donations from individuals in the society with a non-profit motive
